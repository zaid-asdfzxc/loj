# Research Guide

## Priority Research Paths

### Shield Research
   - HP Research (1st)
   - Defense Research (2nd)
   - Attack Research (3rd)
   - Lethality Research (4th)

### Bomber Research
   - Attack Research (1st)
   - Defense Research (2nd)
   - Lethality Research (3rd)
   - HP Research (4th)

### Shooter Research
   - Lethality Research (1st)
   - Attack Research (2nd)
   - HP Research (3rd)
   - Defense Research (4th)
